FAQs:

    How often do me meet?

    We normally try to meet up two to three times a week, and if that isn't possible we will have a group voice call with everyone in the club.

    What are the covid-19 precautions in place?

    We wear masks at all times, even when participating against each other. Balloons are sanitized after use or a new balloon is used. Social distancing is maintained when possible.

    What do I need to bring?

    There is no requirment to bring any equipment as everything you need is provided by the club, all you need is yourself and a water bottle to stay hydrated.

    Can I bring a friend?

    Yes of course! It is in fact encouraged as it helps the club grow and it makes the game even more fun.
