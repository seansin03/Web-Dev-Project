What you need to know:

This website is all about the new hit activity of Balloon keepy uppy. If you are a newcomer to the activity then this website will guide you about what to do and how to do it. Thankfully Balloon Keepy Uppy is very straight forward so there really isn't too much to learn, but we will go through the rules anyway.

How we formed:

The club formed during the Covid lockdowns of 2020 and 2021, when people were stuck at home and had nothing to do, so what better way to pass the time via a fun competitive game of balloon keepy uppy!! The students of MTU then decided to form this club so that students can enjoy this exciting game together. Recently other universities have followed suit so there will also be competitions availible for those to want to prove their keepy uppy skills and represent MTU.

Covid-19 precautions:
